🍪 Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies: Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, analyze website performance, and improve your browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly (e.g., cookie consent preferences, session management)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the website using services like Sentry for error tracking (only with your consent)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings such as dark/light theme preference

Managing Cookies: You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality. You can manage analytics cookies through our cookie consent banner that appears when you first visit the site.

Third-Party Cookies: We use Sentry (sentry.io) for error monitoring when you provide consent. These services may set their own cookies. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

📊 Analytics & Tracking

We use cookies and Google Analytics only with your explicit consent via our cookie banner displayed on your first visit. These tools help us understand how visitors interact with our website and enable us to improve your experience.

Cookies we may use include essential cookies for site functionality, analytics cookies to track usage patterns, and preference cookies to remember your settings. All analytics tracking is configured to respect user privacy with IP anonymization enabled.

You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser storage or adjusting your cookie preferences. You may also use browser settings or third-party tools to block cookies, though this may affect site functionality.

🔒 Security & Content Security Policy (CSP)

This website implements a Content Security Policy (CSP) to enhance security and protect against cross-site scripting (XSS), clickjacking, and other code injection attacks. The CSP restricts the sources from which content can be loaded and executed on this website. Only trusted domains are whitelisted for scripts, styles, fonts, and other resources. This helps ensure that malicious scripts cannot be injected into the website and protects your browsing experience.
