About Me

Passionate developer crafting digital experiences with precision and creativity

I'm Samrath "Sami" Singh, a motivated junior developer from Point Cook, VIC. I have hands-on experience in coding, system design, and client support. At age 14, I registered my own ABN and launched my first business. Since then, I've been building ICT solutions that make a difference.

I create user-focused web applications and digital solutions. I design 3D models for 3DPrintzAU, build custom Minecraft plugins, and develop web apps through Procoders Coding Service. I approach every project with attention to detail and a commitment to quality.

I gather requirements carefully, test thoroughly, and document clearly. I build systems that are functional, secure, well-documented, and genuinely solve problems for users.

I'm currently in Year 9 (age 15) at Point Cook College while running multiple businesses and taking on freelance development work. Next year, I'll be enrolling at Homestead Senior Secondary College for Year 10 to pursue my passion for technology and practical skills. I'm passionate about continuous learning and contributing to impactful projects.

I specialize in custom web development, Minecraft plugin development, eCommerce solutions (especially Shopify), API integration, and automation. I also provide technical support and troubleshooting services.
