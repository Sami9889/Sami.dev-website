About Me

Passionate developer crafting digital experiences with precision and creativity

I'm Samrath "Sami" Singh, a motivated junior developer from Point Cook, VIC with hands-on experience in coding, system design, and client support. My journey started at age 14 when I registered my own ABN and launched my first business, and I've been building ICT solutions ever since.

I specialize in creating user-focused web applications and digital solutions. From designing 3D models for 3DPrintzAU to building custom Minecraft plugins and web apps through Procoders Coding Service, I approach every project with attention to detail and a commitment to quality.

My development philosophy centers around gathering requirements, thorough testing, and clear documentation. I believe in building systems that are not just functional, but secure, well-documented, and truly solve problems for users.

Currently studying at Point Cook College, I balance education with running multiple businesses and freelance development work. I'm passionate about continuous learning and contributing to impactful projects.
